Explanation of Symbols

  • P — Laser power (W)
  • w₀ — Beam waist at focus (μm)
  • λ — Laser wavelength (nm)
  • n — Particle refractive index
  • a — Particle radius (nm)
  • ρ — Particle density (kg/m³)
  • U — Dipole potential (J or kBT)
  • Fgrad — Gradient force (N)
  • Fscat — Scattering force (N)
  • zR — Rayleigh range (μm)
  • α — Polarizability (C·m²/V)
  • Γ — Gas damping rate (rad/s)
  • fax — Axial trap frequency (kHz)
  • fr — Radial trap frequency (kHz)
  • Sx(ω) — Position PSD (m²/Hz)
  • FWHM — Full width at half maximum of PSD peak
